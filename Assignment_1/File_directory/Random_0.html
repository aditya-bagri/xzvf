William Delany

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

William P. Delany (1855 – 7 March 1916)[1] was an Irish Member of Parliament (MP) representing Queen's County Ossory, from 1900 to 1916.[1][2] He was one of the founders of the United Irish League.
