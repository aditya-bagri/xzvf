Independent Talent Group Ltd. (Independent Talent) is the new name of the London-based talent, literary, and model agency formerly known as ICM London.

History[edit]
The newly formed company was originally formed with the merger of talent and literacy agency Duncan Heath Associates and ICM Inc. Duncan Heath Associates was bought out by ICM in 2002 after 33 years of business, becoming ICM (London). In September 2007, ICM (London) became Independent Talent after a management buy out.
Independent Talent has Talent and Literary divisions, as well as Presenters, Commercials, Branding and AdVoice, the voiceover division. [1]
Talent & Literary[edit]
Independent is Europe's largest talent agency. It represents top names in the UK such as actors; Patrick Stewart, Maggie Smith, Ian McKellen, Daniel Craig, Gillian Anderson, Orlando Bloom, Rachel Weisz and Thandie Newton as well as award winning directors; Jonathan Glazer and Sam Mendes.
