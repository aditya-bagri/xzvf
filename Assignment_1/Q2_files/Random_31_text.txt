Fathabad (Persian: فتح اباد‎‎, also Romanized as Fatḩābād)[1] is a village in Hoseynabad Rural District, Esmaili District, Anbarabad County, Kerman Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 289, in 74 families.[2]
