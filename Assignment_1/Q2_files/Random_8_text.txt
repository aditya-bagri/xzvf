Kand-e Qabrestan (Persian: كنده قبرستان‎‎, also Romanized as Kand-e Qabrestān)[1] is a village in Vardasht Rural District, in the Central District of Semirom County, Isfahan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 17, in 4 families.[2]
