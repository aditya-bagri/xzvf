Miętno [ˈmjɛntnɔ] (German: Minten) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Nowogard, within Goleniów County, West Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-western Poland.[1] It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) north of Nowogard, 27 km (17 mi) north-east of Goleniów, and 49 km (30 mi) north-east of the regional capital Szczecin.
Before 1945 the area was part of Germany. For the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
