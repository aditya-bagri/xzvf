E.C.A. Elginkan Anadolu Lisesi (ECAEAL or EAL or usually ECA), is a Boarding school in Maltepe, İstanbul. It has been known by this name since 2008.
History[edit]
Maltepe Middle School[edit]
Maltepe Middle School provided their first graduates in 1962. In the same year Maltepe High School was established to continue the graduates' education.
