The Lone Deranger is a goa trance album released by Hallucinogen in 1997 on Twisted Records. The name of the album is word play on the Lone Ranger, a fictional cowboy and hero of several eponymous radio and television series.

Track listing[edit]

  1. "Demention"
  2. "Snakey Shaker"
  3. "Trancespotter"
  4. "Horrorgram"
  5. "Snarling (Remix)"
  6. "Gamma Goblins Part 2"
  7. "Deranger"
  8. "Jiggle of the Sphinx"
  9. "Synthesizzler" (bonus track in German release)
