Hush (Persian: هوش‎‎, also Romanized as Hūsh)[1] is a village in Miyan Rud Rural District, Qolqol Rud District, Tuyserkan County, Hamadan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 263, in 52 families.[2]
