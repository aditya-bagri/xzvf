Litjens

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

Litjens is a Dutch patronymic surname most common in northern Limburg.[1] Notable people with the surname include:

  • Joey Litjens (born 1990), Dutch motorcyclist
  • Michelle Litjens (born 1972), American (Wisconsin) politician and legislator
  • Paul Litjens (born 1947), Dutch field hockey player
  • Pieter Litjens (born 1968), Dutch politician
  • Stefan Litjens (1913–2002), German WWII pilot
  • Thomas Litjens (born 1984), Dutch footballer
