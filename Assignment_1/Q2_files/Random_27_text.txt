Richmond International High School and College (RIHSC) was a private school in Richmond, British Columbia,[1] founded in 1991.[2] It was Japanese-owned, and catered primarily to foreign students. The school closed in 2005, and the site is currently for sale.[3] A sister school in Tokyo remains in operation.[4]

Academic programs[edit]
The high school offered grades 10 to 12 under the B.C. curriculum.[5]
The college offered:[6]

English language training
A diploma in Computer Science
A diploma in Hospitality and Tourism
A diploma in Business Administration
A Royal Roads University Bachelor of Commerce transfer program
An Acadia University Bachelor of Computer Science transfer program

Trivia[edit]
Scenes of the Fringe episodes "Jacksonville" and "Subject 13" were shot at the school.[7][8]
