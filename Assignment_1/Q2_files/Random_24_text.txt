Rojhuwas is a village in Jatusana Tehsil in Rewari district of Haryana State, India. It belongs to Gurgaon division. It is located 16 km north from District headquarters Rewari, 2 km from Jatusana, and 315 km from State capital Chandigarh. 123401 is its pincode and postal head office is Tehsil Road Rewari. Rohrai, Parkhotampur, Musepur, Gopalpur Gazi, Haluhera are the nearby villages to Rojhuwas.[1]
