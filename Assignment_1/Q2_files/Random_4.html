The Hopeful Stakes is a Listed flat horse race in Great Britain open to horses aged three years or older. It is run on the July Course at Newmarket over a distance of 6 furlongs (1,027 metres), and it is scheduled to take place each year in August.

The race was awarded Listed status in 2002.
