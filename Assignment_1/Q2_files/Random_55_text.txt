Muthialpet is a legislative assembly constituency in the Union territory of Puducherry in India.[1] This constituency covers the area under wards 1–4 and 6–8 of Puducherry Municipality.[1] Muthialpet assembly constituency is a part of Puducherry (Lok Sabha constituency).[1]
