Daveh Shahri (Persian: دوه شهري‎‎, also Romanized as Daveh Shahrī)[1] is a village in Akhtachi-ye Mahali Rural District, Simmineh District, Bukan County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 100, in 25 families.[2]
