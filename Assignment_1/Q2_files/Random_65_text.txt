The following is a list of people assassinated in 1909.

Itō Hirobumi, former Japanese Prime Minister, head of Japanese colonization of Korea when assassinated
William Hutt Curzon Wyllie, former British official in India
Ramón Lorenzo Falcón, Chief of the Argentine Federal Police
Allen Augustus "Gus" Bobbitt, Deputy U.S. Marshal in Oklahoma, assassinated by Jim Miller
