Recea (German: Waywodretschen; Hungarian: Vajdarécse) is a commune in Braşov County, Romania. It is composed of seven villages: Berivoi (Berivoj), Dejani (Dezsán), Gura Văii (Netot), Iaşi (Jás), Recea, Săsciori (Szeszcsor) and Săvăstreni (Szevesztrény).
