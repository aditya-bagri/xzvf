Spyros Christopoulos (Greek: Σπύρος Χριστόπουλος; born 7 October 1975) is a retired Greek footballer who played as a goalkeeper mostly for AEL and Thrasyvoulos in the Greek Super League. He now works as a goalkeepers coach in teams of lower leagues.
