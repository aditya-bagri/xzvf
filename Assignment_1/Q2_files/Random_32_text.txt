Miles Higson (born 27 March 1993) is a British actor, known for playing Seth Costello in the British soap opera Hollyoaks from 2010 to 2011.

Career and life[edit]
He has recently studied for his A-Levels at Bacup and Rawtenstall Grammar School where he also took his GCSEs. He has also attended the Manchester School Of Acting.
Before landing his role in Hollyoaks he appeared in a short film wish All Seeing Eye Productions.[1]
Higson shares a flat with Claire Cooper who plays Jacqui McQueen on the show.[2]
