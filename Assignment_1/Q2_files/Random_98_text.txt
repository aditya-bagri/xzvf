Marwa Barhoumi (born (1988-04-28)28 April 1988) is a Tunisian female volleyball player. She is a member of the Tunisia women's national volleyball team and played for CF Carthage in 2014. She was part of the Tunisian national team at the 2014 FIVB Volleyball Women's World Championship in Italy.[1]
