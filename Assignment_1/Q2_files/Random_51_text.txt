M & O Chevrolet Company is a historic Chevrolet automobile showroom and service center located at Fayetteville, Cumberland County, North Carolina. It was designed by noted architect Frank Benton and built between 1934 and 1937. It is a one-story, Art Deco style building. It has an expansive barrel-vaulted service center and features a vertical central tower and stepped roofline.[2]
It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1983.[1]
