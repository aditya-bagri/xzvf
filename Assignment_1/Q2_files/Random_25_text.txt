Zarośle [zaˈrɔɕlɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Zbiczno, within Brodnica County, Kuyavian-Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-central Poland.[1] It lies 3 kilometres (2 mi) north-west of Zbiczno, 12 km (7 mi) north of Brodnica, and 61 km (38 mi) north-east of Toruń.
