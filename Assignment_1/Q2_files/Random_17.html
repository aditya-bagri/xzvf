1965 Pan Arab Games

Jump to: navigation, search

The 4th Pan Arab Games, a regional multi-sport event held in Arab countries, were held in Cairo, Egypt between September 2 and September 11, 1965. A total number of 1500 athletes from 14 countries participated in events in 12 sports.
