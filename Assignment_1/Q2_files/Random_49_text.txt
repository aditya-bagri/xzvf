John Scott McRoberts (born 14 August 1962 at Scarborough, Ontario) is a SKUD18 classified Canadian Paralympic Sailer who has competed in the 1996, 2008, and 2012 Paralympic Games.[1]
Personal life[edit]
McRoberts was born on 14 September 1962 in Scarborough, Ontario. He resides in Victoria, British Columbia. He became a quadriplegic at 18 years old after diving into shallow water.[1]
