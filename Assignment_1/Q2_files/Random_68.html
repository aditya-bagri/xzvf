Cabezabellosa de la Calzada

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

Cabezabellosa de la Calzada is a village and municipality in the province of Salamanca, western Spain, part of the autonomous community of Castile and León. It is 23 kilometres from the provincial capital city of Salamanca and has a population of 122 people.

It lies 856 metres above sea level.

The postal code is 37490.
