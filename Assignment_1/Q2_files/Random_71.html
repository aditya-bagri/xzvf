Cao Haiqing (Chinese: 曹海清; born 28 September 1993 in Wuhan) is a Chinese footballer who plays for Hangzhou Greentown in the Chinese Super League.

Club career[edit]

Cao Haiqing started his professional football career in 2011 when he was loaned to Wenzhou Provenza's squad for the 2011 China League Two campaign.[1] He was promoted to Hangzhou Greentown first team squad in 2012 by Takeshi Okada.[2] He made his Super League debut on 27 May 2012 against Henan Jianye, coming on as a substitute for Kim Dong-jin in the 70th minute.[3]
