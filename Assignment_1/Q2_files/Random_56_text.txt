Zeyt-e Sofla (Persian: زيت سفلي‎‎, also Romanized as Zeyt-e Soflá; also known as Pā’īn Zeyd and Zeyt-e Pā’īn)[1] is a village in Miandorud-e Bozorg Rural District, in the Central District of Miandorud County, Mazandaran Province, Iran.[2] At the 2006 census, its population was 1,610, in 446 families.[3]
