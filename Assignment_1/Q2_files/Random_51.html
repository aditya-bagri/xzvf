Janówek [jaˈnuvɛk] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Żelechów, within Garwolin County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland.[1] It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) south-west of Żelechów, 22 km (14 mi) south-east of Garwolin, and 78 km (48 mi) south-east of Warsaw.

The village has a population of 153.
