Fantasmagoria is an Argentine rock band featuring Gori" on vocals and acoustic guitar, Mariano Acosta on vocals and piano, and Augusto Giannoni on drums.
The band was formed in Argentina in 2000 when Gori left punk rock band Fun People and began working on his own project. He replaced the harsh sound of his electric guitar with an acoustic one, giving the group its distinctive sound.
Fantasmagoria currently has two EPs, called "Fantasmagoria" and "Clearance", and three albums, entitled "Atravesando el camino (que nos lleva a los otros caminos)", "Abracadabra", and "El Rio", respectively.
