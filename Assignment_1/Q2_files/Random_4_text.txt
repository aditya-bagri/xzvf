Smith (first name and dates unknown) was an English first-class cricketer associated with Surrey and Godalming Cricket Club who was active in the 1820s and is recorded in two matches from 1821 to 1822, totalling 25 runs with a highest score of 20. He represented the Players in an early Gentlemen v Players match.[1]
