Šutna (pronounced [ˈʃuːtna]) is a settlement east of Podbočje in the Municipality of Krško in eastern Slovenia. The area was traditionally part of Lower Carniola. It is now included with the rest of the municipality in the Lower Sava Statistical Region.[2]
