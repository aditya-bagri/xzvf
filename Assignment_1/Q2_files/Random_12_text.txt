Neaz Morshed Nahid is a First class and List A cricketer from Bangladesh. A right-arm fast-medium bowler, he is sometimes referred to on scoresheets by his nickname Nahid and played for Dhaka Division in 2004/05. He took 3 for 23 against Rajshahi Division in one of his two first class games and 4 for 24 against Khulna Division in a limited overs clash.
