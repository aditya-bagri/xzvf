2,7,4'-Trihydroxyisoflavanone 4'-O-methyltransferase (EC 2.1.1.212, SAM:2,7,4'-trihydroxyisoflavanone 4'-O-methyltransferase, HI4'OMT, HMM1, MtIOMT5) is an enzyme with system name S-adenosyl-L-methionine:2,7,4'-trihydroxyisoflavanone 4'-O-methyltransferase .[1][2][3][4] This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

S-adenosyl-L-methionine + 2,7,4'-trihydroxyisoflavanone S-adenosyl-L-homocysteine + 2,7-dihydroxy-4'-methoxyisoflavanone

This enzyme specifically methylates 2,7,4'-trihydroxyisoflavanone on the 4'-position.
