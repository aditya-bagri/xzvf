Peda-bayalu is a village and a Mandal in Visakhapatnam district in the state of Andhra Pradesh in India.[1]
Peda Bayalu mandal is bordered by Munchingi Puttu, Hukumpeta, Paderu and Gangaraju Madugula mandals of Visakhapatnam district, and by Odisha state.
