Buddleja davidii 'Malvern Blue' is a British cultivar raised at the Southcombe Nursery, Kewton, near Exeter. [1]
Description[edit]
'Malvern Blue' is distinguished by its panicles of deep purple-blue flowers with pale blue eyes and orange throats. The foliage is narrow, dark green above and grey-green below. [1]
