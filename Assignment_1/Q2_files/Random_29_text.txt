Kowalewice [kɔvalɛˈvit͡sɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Darłowo, within Sławno County, West Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-western Poland.[1] It lies approximately 10 kilometres (6 mi) east of Darłowo, 12 km (7 mi) north-west of Sławno, and 173 km (107 mi) north-east of the regional capital Szczecin.
Before 1945 the area was part of Germany. For the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
The village has a population of 184.
