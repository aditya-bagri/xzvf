Claudia Dasca Romeu (born 4 November 1994 in Sabadell) is a Spanish swimmer who competes in the Women's 400m individual medley.[1] At the 2012 Summer Olympics she finished 25th overall in the heats in the Women's 400 metre individual medley and failed to reach the final.
