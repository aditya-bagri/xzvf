Mount Hope Township is located in McLean County, Illinois. As of the 2010 census, its population was 1,103 and it contained 455 housing units.[1]
Geography[edit]
According to the 2010 census, the township has a total area of 48.79 square miles (126.4 km2), of which 48.77 square miles (126.3 km2) (or 99.96%) is land and 0.02 square miles (0.052 km2) (or 0.04%) is water.[1]
