José Joaquín Casasús (1733–1822) was a Spanish writer.
Life[edit]
Born in Alcira, José Joaquín Casasús was the son of José Javier Casasús Judici de Echarte, knight of the Order of Montesa, and of Margarita de Navía-Osorio y Roig, daughter of the marquess of Santa Cruz de Marcenado. He attended Colegio de San Pablo de Valencia, where he studied Latin, French, and philosophy.
